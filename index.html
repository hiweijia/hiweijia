About me

Hi! I am a multimedia interactive designer currently based in France. I graduated from the China Academy of Art and furthered my education with a master's degree at the Media Lab of L'École de design Nantes Atlantique in France. The integration of media design and spatial design has enriched my academic journey, making me 60% a media designer, 40% a spatial designer, and 100% an explorer who sets no limits for myself.

My current research is focused on exploring the dynamics between interpersonal relationships. I am not satisfied with merely being an observer of society; instead, I actively immerse myself within it, using my creativity as a means of expression and engagement. I am dedicated to becoming someone who can spark discussions, identify sensitive issues, and use design as a tool to facilitate dialogue and social change. In this process, I am also exploring the meaning of my own existence, attempting to reflect on and understand the interactions and influences between individuals and society through my work and actions.
